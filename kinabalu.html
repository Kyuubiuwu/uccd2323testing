KOTA KINABALU

Nestled on the northwest coast of Borneo, Kota Kinabalu, affectionately known as "KK," is a dynamic city that beautifully blends its rich history with modern aspirations. It serves as the gateway to Sabah's natural wonders, offering visitors a taste of both urban excitement and pristine wilderness.
