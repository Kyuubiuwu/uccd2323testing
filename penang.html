PENANG

Penang, fondly known as the "Pearl of the Orient," is a captivating state on the northwest coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Comprising Penang Island and a slice of the mainland called Seberang Perai, it's a vibrant melting pot of cultures, a treasure trove of history, and a haven for food lovers.
