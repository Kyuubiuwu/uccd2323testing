KUALA LUMPUR

Kuala Lumpur, or KL as it's affectionately called, is a city that pulsates with energy. It's the capital of Malaysia, a bustling metropolis where towering skyscrapers stand side-by-side with historic landmarks, and where diverse cultures blend harmoniously.
